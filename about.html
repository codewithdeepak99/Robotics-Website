Our Story

Who We Are

The Robotics Club of NIT Patna has been an embodiment of innovation since its inception in 2015–16 under the guidance of Mr. Nripendra Saroj. Our club carries a proud legacy of excellence, collaboration, and technological exploration.

Our members range from seasoned programmers and creative designers to hardware enthusiasts. We have represented NIT Patna at prestigious platforms such as the Smart India Hackathon.

Our Mission

To continuously strive to enhance students’ technical skills while nurturing curiosity in the fascinating world of robotics, automation, and electronics.

What We Do

Ever wondered how robots think, move, or make decisions? We blend mechanical design, electronics, programming, and AI to turn curiosity into reality.

We believe in learning by building. Together, we work, compete, and evolve.
